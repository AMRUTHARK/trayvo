## Cost Breakdown

### Infrastructure Costs

#### **Option 1: Free Tier (Recommended for Start)**
- **Frontend (Vercel):** ₹0/month
  - Free tier: Unlimited projects, 100GB bandwidth
  - Sufficient for 1-5 customers
  
- **Backend (Render):** ₹0/month
  - Free tier: Spins down after 15 min inactivity
  - Good for development/testing
  - May need upgrade for production reliability

- **Database (PlanetScale):** ₹0/month
  - Free tier: 1 database, 1GB storage, 1 billion rows
  - Sufficient for multiple shops

- **Email (Gmail SMTP):** ₹0/month
  - Free: 500 emails/day
  - Sufficient for invitations and notifications

- **Domain (Optional):** ₹150/month
  - Custom domain for professional appearance
  - Optional but recommended

**Total Free Tier:** ₹0-150/month

- **Frontend (Vercel Pro):** ₹0/month (Free tier sufficient)
- **Backend (Render):** ₹700/month (~$7/month)
  - Always-on service
  - Better performance
  - No spin-down delays
  
- **Database (PlanetScale):** ₹0/month (Free tier sufficient)
- **Email (SendGrid):** ₹0/month (Free tier: 100 emails/day)
- **Domain:** ₹150/month

**Total Paid Tier:** ₹850/month
